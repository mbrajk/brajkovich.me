Mike Brajkovich
Summary

Mike Brajkovich is a software developer with over four years of professional experience. Throughout his career his main focus has been back-end development using C# and the .NET framework as well as front-end web-development using mostly Javascript, HTML/CSS, etc. He is very comfortable working on collaborative, small-team projects and is capable of jumping into new technologies and environments without skipping a beat.

Mike Brajkovich obtained both his Bachelor of Science and Master's degree in Computer Science from Southern Illinois University Carbondale in Illinois. He has previously worked for CDM Smith as a full-stack developer, designing and creating custom web-portals that replaced paper-based business processes. He is currently working for a start-up called R&B Soft that provides scheduling, training and staffing software for the medical industry.
